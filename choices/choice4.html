You got... the lemon tree!

**Characteristics:**
helpful and revitalizing

**Greek Myth Interpretation:**
In a myth involving Athena, the writer describes how lemons trees were “Happy to be a favorite and useful tree for everyone”

**Book Connection:**
The lemon tree represents Teacake, who becomes apart of Janie’s “rebirth” after Jody dies, being a beacon of energy for everyone he is surrounded with

**Quote:**
“Tea Cake fell in beside her and mounted the porch this time. So she offered him a seat and they made a lot of laughter out of nothing. Near eleven o’clock she remembered a piece of pound cake she had put away. Tea Cake went out to the lemon tree at the corner of the kitchen and picked some lemons and squeezed them for her. So they had lemonade too” (101).
